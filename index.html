BrainetLab is an independent research line of the Center for Neuroscience and Cognitive Systems of Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia, hosted at the University of Trento. The Center for Neuroscience and Cognitive Systems (CNCS) of IIT@UniTn focuses on a multidisciplinary approach to understanding the computational abilities of the brain.
